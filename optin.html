Photo Campaign SMS Opt-In

You can choose to receive SMS messages that request a one-time photo submission for a specific project or campaign.

How to opt in

Text START to +1XXX-XXX-XXXX from your mobile phone.

By texting START, you agree to receive limited, non-recurring SMS messages requesting a photo submission for a specific project.

You can reply HELP at any time for more information.
