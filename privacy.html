Privacy Policy

This application is a private tool used to request and collect photos from known contacts as part of specific, one-time campaigns.

Information Collected

  • Phone numbers of contacts who have explicitly opted in
  • Photos voluntarily sent by contacts
  • Campaign-related metadata (timestamps, campaign names)

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to organize and review photos submitted by contacts for the intended campaign. Data is not sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes.

Opt-In and Consent

Contacts are only messaged after providing explicit consent, either verbally or in writing, and are informed of the purpose of the request.

Opt-Out

Contacts may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
